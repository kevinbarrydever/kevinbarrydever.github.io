About

A trusted name in building & construction sales

For more than three decades, Kevin Barry Dever has helped manufacturers and dealers in the tile, flooring, stone, kitchen & bath, and construction products industries grow their businesses. From founding a fast-scaling company recognized five years running on the Inc. 500 list, to running his own independent manufacturers' agency, his career is defined by durable relationships, disciplined execution, and a reputation for integrity.

Colleagues and clients describe him as an elite sales professional who can speak just as comfortably to senior executives as to the shop floor — always guiding decisions toward what's best for the customer.
